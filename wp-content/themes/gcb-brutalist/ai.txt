## Description
Gay Car Boys is Australia's leading LGBTQI+ automotive publication. We provide independent car reviews, news, and lifestyle content at the intersection of car culture and queer culture. Founded by Alan Zurvas in 2008, GCB offers honest, irreverent, and critically independent automotive journalism with a proudly queer lens.

## Content Scope
- Australian new car reviews (all segments: SUV, sedan, EV, PHEV, ute, sports)
- Emerging Chinese automotive brands (BYD, Chery, GWM, Jaecoo, Omoda, Deepal, Zeekr, Leapmotor)
- Electric vehicle and PHEV transition coverage
- Australian market pricing, specifications, and ANCAP safety ratings
- Motorsport coverage (Supercars, F1, rally)
- LGBTQI+ automotive lifestyle (road trips, festivals, culture)
- Car buying guides and comparisons

## Editorial Voice
GCB is known for direct, honest, and often irreverent automotive journalism. We champion value and innovation over brand loyalty, with particular focus on how emerging brands are disrupting legacy automakers in the Australian market.
